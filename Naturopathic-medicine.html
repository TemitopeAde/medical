Naturopathic Medicine

Naturopathic physicians believe that the body has an innate capacity to heal itself. Naturopathic physicians blend integrative medicine and natural therapies, treating each person holistically, tapping a person's own internal body, mind and spirit resources to improve health and treat illness. Naturopathic medicine is a distinct system of primary health care-an art, science and practice of preventing, diagnosing and treating conditions of the human mind and body.

Naturopathy uses a blend of different therapies, all in order to restore the body's health in the most natural, non-invasive way possible by stimulating the body's own healing power.

Naturopathic Doctors use 6 Principles:
  1. Nature has the power to heal
  2. Treat the cause, not the effect
  3. Treat the whole person
  4. Do no harm
  5. Encourage prevention
  6. Act as a teacher
Naturopathic physicians can provide:
  • Preventive and wellness care and chronic disease management
  • A holistic approach to healing the whole person
  • Annual comprehensive holistic history and physical
  • Diet and lifestyle evaluation
  • Comprehensive lab testing
  • A partner with you to create a treatment plan
