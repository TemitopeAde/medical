About:

Coy Roskosky, D.C., Doctor of Chiropractic, is a leading chiropractic doctor in the Washington, D.C., Maryland and Virginia (DMV) metro area for pain treatment, musculoskeletal and neurological health issues, sports injuries and performance.

Trained in California, Dr. Coy uses a two-pronged approach to healthcare which includes looking at how the whole body works and how it affects function of the body. He provides an effective and progressive treatment approach to improving health and the overall enjoyment of life.

Dr. Coy recommends care of yourself through combinations of exercise, rest, good energy, physical maintenance, nutrition and balance. It is vital to look at these aspects of the entire body first in order to unlock the underlying causes of health problems.

“Function” is the second part to address when it comes to health. The body may work, but does it function to its full potential or is it slowly breaking down, causing us to lose movement and function? Sometimes people ignore the tension in the body that leads to pain and discomfort. Intervention and preventative care can get you moving in the right direction to a healthy life.

Dr. Coy is dedicated to providing patients with integrity, knowledge and care so they experience relief from pain and longer lasting results. His mission is to find the root cause of your pain and dysfunction and correct it, so you can truly heal and feel better and whole again.
