Approach:

Dr. Kannankeril spends time with her patients to truly listen and to make connections with each of a patient’s symptoms. She uses that information along with appropriate testing to get to the bottom of each case and develop a comprehensive treatment plan. She is able to prescribe medications, but sees the value in using a natural approach as a first line of treatment.
Dr. Kannankeril practices Naturopathic Medicine, a natural approach to health and healing that recognizes the whole person. This form of medicine has been studied and practiced for hundreds of years and works with the following principles of healing:

Prevention
The goal of any health care should be prevention. Dr. Kannankeril teaches her patients tools for living a healthy life.
First do no harm
Dr. Kannankeril uses the least invasive treatments to minimize risks of harmful side effects.
The physician as teacher
Dr. Kannankeril works to create a healthy, sensitive relationship with her patients. She educates them about their health and encourages self-responsibility in their healing processes
Identify and treat the cause
While modern medicine works to suppress symptoms, Dr.Kannankeril works to find and remove the underlying causes of illness.
Treat the whole person
Any imbalance in the body, mind or spirit affects the entire person. Dr. Kannankeril takes numerous factors into account when creating her personalized and comprehensive treatments.
The healing power of nature
Dr. Kannankeril's role is to remove obstacles to health by assisting the body's natural healing process.

About:

Dr. Kannankeril is a licensed, board certified Doctor of Naturopathic Medicine. She is trained in natural medicine therapies including clinical nutrition, herbal medicine and nutritional supplements. While trained in the standard medical model and pharmaceutical drugs, she often works with her patients to wean them off unnecessary medications that can carry harmful side effects or prescribes compounded medications customized to meet her patients' health needs.

Dr. Kannankeril received her bachelor’s degree from George Washington University in Washington, DC. While pre-med, she became interested in natural forms of healing and went on to study homeopathy and Ayurvedic medicine near her family home in Kerala, India and then pursued a Master’s degree in Natural Health. She completed her naturopathic medical degree at the National College of Naturopathic Medicine in Portland, Oregon, the oldest accredited naturopathic medical school in the country. Dr. Kannankeril also received certification in homeopathy and IV Therapy, completed internships in Cardiopulmonary Medicine and Women’s Health as well as a residency in Integrative Medicine.

Dr. Kannankeril was originally appointed by the former Mayor Anthony Williams as a member of the DC Board of Medicine's Advisory Committee on Naturopathic Medicine in 2006. She was reappointed to this position and made Chairperson of the Committee by Mayor Adrian Fenty in 2009. As Chairperson, she regularly consults with the Board of Medicine on integrative medicine and is a member of the American Association of Naturopathic Physicians.
