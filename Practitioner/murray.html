About Ron Murray

Ron Murray holds doctorates in Integrative Medicine and Traditional Naturopathy and is a licensed physical therapist with over 20 years of experience. He has additional certifications and expertise in a number of different modalities, including craniosacral therapy, heart rate variability, visceral manipulation, myofascial release. He is the developer of the ground-breaking Manual Regulation Therapy method.

Ron is the director of the Asclepeion Center of Body Mind Therapy and served on the faculty of Capitol University of Integrative Medicine for 10 years where he taught the Colloid Fluid Model approach to craniosacral therapy. His mentors include Dr. John Upledger who among others taught him advanced forms of body-mind work; Maria Gabrielle and Dr. Bradford who mentored him in Darkfield Microscopy; as well as Dr. Konrad Werthmann, and Dr. Thomas Rau, founder of the Paracelsus Clinic in Switzerland.

Manual Regulation Therapy (MRT)

Manual Regulation Therapy (MRT) is a synthesis of the modalities and understanding that Ron Murray has acquired through his training in a variety of therapies. This therapeutic method integrates manual, body and mind therapies, including conventional physical therapy, CranioSacral therapy, visceral manipulation, and myofascial release, as well as biological and energy medicine. MRT is evidence-based medicine, and as been extensively validated through the use of Heart Rate Variability (HRV), which is a widely recognized measure of autonomic nervous system function. Mr. Murray is the author of “A Colloid Fluid Model: The Bridge Between Biological and Energy Medicine,” published internationally in Explore magazine, and a pioneer of Manual Regulation Therapy.
